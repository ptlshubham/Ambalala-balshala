History
Ambalal Balshala is run by the Charotar Education Society

and is one of the most well-known and respected educational institutions in Gujarat. Children's development in all areas and education are the primary goals.Â 

Motilal Amin Saheb, who was the creator of the Charotar Education Society, started the first Gujarati school in 1919. It was inaugurated on September 13. 9-11-1926 "Ambalal Balshala" was named in memory of self-helper Ambalal Motibhai Patel.

Self-employed Mr. Ambalal Motibhai was born in Sojitra. His birth date was 17-9-1890. He studied B.A. and he passed away from the plague. It occurred on June 2, 1918. The plague epidemic was a major issue, and many people died from the illness instead of serving their fellow citizens or were executed.
